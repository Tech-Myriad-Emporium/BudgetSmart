The family budget app that teaches money, not just tracks it

Most budgeting apps treat a family like one shared spreadsheet. BudgetSmart Family gives every member — partner, teens, kids — their own wallet inside your plan, with you in control.

Built for how families actually handle money

  • Up to 5 people — invite members by email; they join your plan automatically
  • Allowance wallets — you add money in; members choose to spend or invest it, and learn the difference
  • Chores that pay — assign "take out the trash, $5, weekly"; marking it done pays their wallet instantly, no nagging bookkeeping
  • Purchase approvals — a kid requests the $25 game, you approve or decline, and approved purchases come out of their wallet
  • Family overview — wallets, allowances given, spending and investing across the whole household at a glance
