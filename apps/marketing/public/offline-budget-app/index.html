The offline budget app for people who read privacy policies

Most budget apps demand your bank login on day one and stream your transactions to their cloud. BudgetSmart is built the opposite way: it’s an offline-first budget app where your financial data lives on your device.

What local-first actually means

  • Works fully offline — budgets, goals, debt, investments, reports: all computed on-device. Airplane mode changes nothing.
  • No bank credentials — nothing to hand over, nothing to breach. Import statement files (CSV/OFX/QFX/QIF) whenever you choose.
  • Local encryption & biometric login — plus an incognito mode for over-the-shoulder moments.
  • Your account is only for your subscription — the central account verifies your plan and sends optional monthly email recaps; it never receives your transactions.

Private doesn’t mean primitive

The smart features run locally too: auto-categorization learned from your own history, duplicate and refund detection, subscription tracking with price-creep alerts, 90-day cashflow forecasting, debt payoff optimization and tax estimation — all without your data leaving the machine.

The Base tier is free forever. Paid tiers (from $5/mo) add the automation. Windows, Linux (AppImage/deb/apt) and Android, in 9 languages.
