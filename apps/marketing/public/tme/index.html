TME — Tech Myriad Emporium

TME stands for Tech Myriad Emporium — the studio behind BudgetSmart, budgeting software that puts your entire financial life in one crisp, private, local-first app. That's why the official BudgetSmart site lives at budgetsmarttme.com: BudgetSmart + TME.

What TME builds

BudgetSmart is TME's flagship: budgets and safe-to-spend, bank statement import with smart auto-categorization, savings goals, debt payoff planning, investment tracking, net worth, cashflow forecasting, tax intelligence, monthly email recaps, and family plans for up to five people with chores, allowances and purchase approvals.

What TME believes

  • Your data is yours. BudgetSmart is local-first — financial data stays on your device, encrypted, and works offline. We never see your transactions.
  • Free should mean free. The Base app is free forever, not a trial.
  • Software should earn its subscription. Paid tiers exist for ongoing value — automation, forecasting, tax intelligence — not for unlocking basics.
