Budgeting software that replaces five apps

BudgetSmart is budgeting software built around one idea: your whole financial life in one place. Instead of a budget app plus a subscription tracker plus a debt calculator plus an investment spreadsheet, you get budgets and a real-time safe-to-spend number, savings goals, debt payoff planning, investing, a true net worth, and full tax intelligence — in one fast, keyboard-friendly desktop app.

What sets it apart from other budgeting software

  • Statement import that thinks. Drop in a CSV, OFX/QFX or QIF export from any bank — BudgetSmart auto-categorizes from your own history, catches duplicates, and matches refunds so your trends stay honest.
  • Local-first privacy. Your financial data lives on your device, not our servers. No bank passwords required, works offline.
  • Forecasting, not just tracking. A 90-day cashflow projection, payday-paced daily allowance, sinking funds for annual bills, and alerts when a subscription quietly raises its price.
  • Tax intelligence. Federal liability projection, quarterly estimates, withholding checks, capital-gains modeling and a deduction finder — the stuff people pay advisors for.
  • Family plans for up to 5. Shared wallets, chores that pay allowance automatically, and purchase approvals.
